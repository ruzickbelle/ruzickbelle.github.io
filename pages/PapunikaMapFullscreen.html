    FAQ

    How can I enter a zone?

    » Left-click on the corresponding zone

    How can I leave a zone?

    » Right-Click

    How do I enter another zone within a zone?

    » Click on the blue text next to the portals to change the zone.

    What does the ‘#’ on the markers mean?

    » The # and the number next to it indicate the position in the Adventure’s Tome. The order does not matter.

    What do the numbers in brackets at the markers mean?

    » The numbers, e.g. (3/4), indicate the order in which something must be done.

    What do I have to do with an ingredient?

    » If you have collected all the ingredients, go to a cook and craft the food.
